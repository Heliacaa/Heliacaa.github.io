Istanbul Transit Analytics

End-to-end data mining pipeline analyzing 96M+ hourly public transit records representing 1.1B passenger events. The resulting paper was presented at ICHORA 2026 and published on IEEE Xplore.

  • Reached 81.14% classification accuracy and R2 = 0.9272 in demand regression.
  • Used SARIMA forecasting with 5.83% MAPE, K-Means clustering, and Apriori association mining.
